We Will Ask Soon. Not Yet.

Phoenix Pride Project is actively pursuing 501(c)(3) status. Until that is approved, we cannot offer tax-deductible receipts. We believe in doing this right from the start.

Why We Are Not Taking Donations Yet

Our Year 1 budget goal is $27,600. That covers peer navigator training, insurance, a simple website, quarterly workshops, and the first Community Pride Circles. When we are ready to fundraise, we will be transparent about every dollar.

In the meantime, the best way to support us is to join our email list, share our name, and show up.
