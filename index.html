3P

Rising Together for LGBTQ+ Young Adults in Las Vegas

Phoenix Pride Project — or 3P, as our community calls us — is a new nonprofit forming in Las Vegas to empower LGBTQ+ young adult survivors of trafficking and those escaping sex work for survival. We are not yet a 501(c)(3), but we are building. And we are not building alone.

Right now, we are laying the foundation: recruiting our board, training peer navigators, and building partnerships with the organizations already doing good work in Clark County. We do not promise what we cannot deliver. We promise to show up, to listen, and to connect young adults to the safety, affirmation, and resources they deserve.

What We Are Building

These are not programs we are running today. They are the vision we are working toward, shaped by survivors, advocates, and the real gaps we see in Las Vegas.

🧘

Healing & Wellness

Trauma-informed care, peer support circles, and identity-affirming mental health navigation.

🏠

Housing & Safety

Emergency shelter referrals, transitional housing pathways, and safety planning for young adults in crisis.

🎓

Education & Employment

Scholarship support (coming Year 2), job readiness training, and connections to affirming employers.

Scholarships are planned for Year 2, pending 501(c)(3) status and program infrastructure.

🤝

Leadership & Community

Survivor-led mentorship, peer navigator training, and monthly Community Pride Circles.

📢

Outreach & Prevention

Street outreach, Know Your Rights workshops, and peer-led prevention education.

Our First Step: The Phoenix Navigation Network

We are launching with one thing done well: the Phoenix Navigation Network.

Five trained volunteer peer navigators. A warm referral system. Quarterly Know Your Rights workshops. Monthly Community Pride Circles for connection and healing.

No building. No bureaucracy. Just people who care, connecting young adults to housing, legal aid, mental health resources, and community. If you are a young adult who needs help, or an organization that wants to partner, we are here.

We Are Building Our Board — Join Us

Phoenix Pride Project is actively recruiting our inaugural board of directors. We need facilitators, financial stewards, detail-oriented secretaries, and at-large directors with legal, development, clinical, or lived experience.

We are especially seeking at least one LGBTQ+ identified member and at least one person with lived experience of trafficking, homelessness, or survival sex work. This board will shape who we become. If you want to help build a nonprofit from the ground up, we want to talk.
